About Us

Our Vision

Gnord Strular is a leading manufacturer of custom cabinetry and wardrobe solutions based in China. We specialize in creating high-quality, custom-made modular furniture for clients across India, Russia, the Middle East, Europe, Australia, and the United States. Our focus on excellence and attention to detail has made us a trusted partner for interior designers, furniture retailers, and homeowners worldwide. We use advanced manufacturing techniques and state-of-the-art equipment to deliver products that exceed expectations.

We believe that premium interior solutions should not be a luxury of the few, but a standard for the modern home. Specializing in modular kitchens, bespoke wardrobes, and integrated cabinetry, we provide whole-house customization that balances high-end European aesthetics with the unparalleled efficiency of advanced factory-direct manufacturing.

Engineering Excellence

Our products are more than furniture; they are precision-engineered systems. Sourced from our state-of-the-art production facilities in China, every cabinet is a result of CNC-driven accuracy and meticulous material selection.

  • Eco-Conscious Standards: We exclusively utilize E0 and ENF-grade formaldehyde-free boards to ensure a healthy indoor environment.
  • Global Hardware Integration: By partnering with industry leaders like Blum, Hettich, Higold and DTC, we guarantee that every hinge, runner, and lift system performs flawlessly for a lifetime.
  • Surface Innovation: From anti-fingerprint PET films to 7-layer piano lacquer paint finishes, our surfaces are tested for moisture resistance, heat stability, and scratch durability.

The Gnord Strular Difference: Modular Flexibility

Unlike traditional "off-the-shelf" retailers, we offer a fully customizable design ecosystem. Whether you are looking for a minimalist Japandi kitchen, a transitional Shaker-style pantry, or a luxury floor-to-ceiling wine display, our modular components can be adapted to any architectural layout.

We bridge the gap between bespoke carpentry and industrial scale, offering you:

  • Direct-to-Consumer Value: Eliminating middle-man markups through a streamlined supply chain.
  • Design-First Approach: A massive library of 100+ textures, wood grains, and sintered stone finishes.
  • Global Reliability: Expertly packed and shipped systems designed for easy on-site assembly and perfect alignment.

Our Commitment

Based on the principles of Gnord (representing strength and northern minimalist roots) and Strular (structure and modularity), our mission is to provide architects, designers, and homeowners with a seamless interior solution that is as functional as it is beautiful.
