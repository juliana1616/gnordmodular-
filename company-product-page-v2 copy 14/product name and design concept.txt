Product 1: Vellano

The Vellano series captures the serene essence of Chino Hills, California, drawing inspiration from its sweeping "far hill" vistas and warm sandstone textures.

Characterized by a "warm contemporary" aesthetic, this collection balances an organized, executive modern look with a polished finish that exudes success. The use of light wood tones cultivates an atmosphere of relaxed luxury, ensuring every space feels both sophisticated and inviting.

Product 2: Japandi

The Japandi series artfully combines Japanese minimalism with Scandinavian functionality. The walls, doors and furniture feature an intentional silhouette, favoring soft colors and lines. This focus on refined geometry is reflected in the sophisticated shelving and structured frames.
A soft palette of cream and white light tones creates an atmosphere that feels both expensive and deeply calming. By blending these two worlds, the design achieves a "quiet luxury" aesthetic, where the organized elegance provides a serene and polished environment for the modern home.


Product style 3: San Carlo
The San Carlo series pays homage to the Teatro Reale di San Carlo, the world's oldest continuously active opera house. This design is elegantly adorned with neoclassical motifs and textures and colors that echo the grandeur of the historic venue. Mirroring the theater's architectural depth, the series borrows the organic curves and wood-centric focus of the 1950s era as well - it scales and "glams" them up for a 21st-century luxury home. This sophisticated fusion is captured in the intricate woodwork and the majestic, organized display.


Product  style 4:  Loire Vally

The Loire Valley series is inspired by France's storied vineyards and rich historical heritage. This design elegantly pairs classical arches with natural stone and deep-toned woods.
It celebrates a "belvedere" lifestyle—blending majestic, elevated aesthetics with a grounded, refined palette. By integrating these timeless elements, the series brings the sophisticated spirit of the Loire Valley into the modern home


Product  style 5：Cairo 
The Cairo series draws inspiration from the Museum of Cairo and the grandeur of Egyptian heritage, reimagined for a modern era. The cabinetry showcases the signature “arched motif" translating classical Roman architecture into tall, slender, and rhythmic woodwork. This is complemented by fluted wall features that offer a subtle, contemporary take on the "ribbed" columns of Gilded Age mansions. Furniture pieces, such as the curved sofa, appear "sculpted" rather than built, intentionally avoiding sharp corners. The inclusion of cane and rattan in seating adds a warm, organic touch to the sophisticated layout. Veined marble islands and backsplashes provide a sense of "gravity," while herringbone wooden floors offer a historic, high-end foundation.



Product 6: Montpelier 
The Montpelier series is a masterclass in architectural balance, emphasizing sleek verticality through floor-to-ceiling modular shelving. This transparent design acts as a sophisticated room divider, allowing the space to breathe while maintaining a gallery-like atmosphere. The collection relies on a rich material palette, pairing high-quality walnut wood paneling and cognac leather with "cool" dark brown powder-coated metal accents brings contrast and warmth. Integrated cove lighting along the ceiling provides a soft, atmospheric radiance, perfectly complementing the oversized, tiered pendant light that serves as a sculptural focal point. Neutral walls and light stone flooring, as seen in the backdrop allow the deeper wood tones and black accents to pop with modern elegance. This series successfully balances "warm" organic textures with sharp architectural lines, a design language echoed in the precise organisation.

product 7： Hudson Valley 
The Hudson Valley series is inspired by the opulent gilded-age estates and country homes of industrial titans like the Rockefellers and Vanderbilts，built along the Hudson River. This style honors the structural integrity and historical weight of those grand Upstate New York mansions, artfully filtering them through a crisp, 21st-century lens.
The theme captures the sophisticated essence of a contemporary villa or apartment — where historic "bones," such as the traditional cabinetry meet trendy, sculptural furniture. The result is a design that feels both anchored in history and vibrantly modern, reflected in the elegant symmetry and arched motifs.

Products 8.  Santorini

The Santorini series draws its inspiration from the breathtaking Aegean coast, utilizing a palette of stark whites and stunning blues to evoke a cool, calm environment.
Defined by ultra-modern minimalism, this design features clean, handle-less geometry and sharp vertical lines that create a "frictionless" visual experience. The use of "push-to-open" cabinets removes visual clutter. This hallmark of contemporary interior design seamlessly blends high-end luxury with a youthful, modern demeanor.



product 9：Montjuïc hill
The Montjuïc Hill series captures the essence of a high-end renovated apartment in Paris or Milan, where historic "bones" are preserved to support a strictly modern lifestyle.
Sitting at a masterful "middle ground," this style balances the clean, walnut-heavy aesthetics of Warm Contemporary with the formal structure of Modern Neoclassical. It embraces the comfort of traditional design but "silences" it through modern color palettes and integrated lighting. As seen in the soft, atmospheric glow and the refined, organized glass displays, the series creates a warm, sophisticated environment that feels both timeless and current.
