San Carlo

Design Concept

The San Carlo series pays homage to the Teatro Reale di San Carlo, the world's oldest continuously active opera house. This design is elegantly adorned with neoclassical motifs and bas-relief sculptures that echo the grandeur of the historic venue.
Mirroring the theater's architectural depth, the series features a rich, deep-colored facade with heavily rusticated textures and an Ionic colonnade. While it borrows the organic curves and wood-centric focus of the 1950s, it scales and "glams" them up for a 21st-century luxury home. This sophisticated fusion is captured in the intricate woodwork and the majestic, organized display.
