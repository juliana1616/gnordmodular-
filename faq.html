🏭 About Our Factory & Sourcing

Where is your factory located? Can we visit?

We are located in Foshan, Guangdong province, China—the global capital of furniture manufacturing. We welcome both B2B partners and private homeowners to visit our facilities. If you cannot travel, we offer live WhatsApp video tours to walk you through our production lines and material showrooms.

What is your production capacity for large projects?

Our manufacturing facility produces over 11,000 SQM of cabinetry every single day. Whether you need a single bespoke kitchen or modular work for a luxury villa or 500 units for a commercial residential development, we have the heavy-duty machinery and scale to handle massive volumes effortlessly.

🤖 Manufacturing & Technology

How do you ensure high precision and quality in your cabinets?

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art industrial robots and a fully automatic manufacturing process. This "Industry 4.0" setup ensures every cut, drill hole, and edge-banding is precise to the millimeter, eliminating the human error found in traditional workshops.

🛡️ Our 100% Quality & Service Commitment

What is your Quality Control (QC) process and guarantee?

We have a rigorous multi-stage QC process. In the rare event of a mistake from our side (such as incorrect dimensions or manufacturing defects), we stand by our work with a 100% replacement guarantee. We will replace the affected units at no additional cost to you.

🪵 Materials & Customization

What materials do you use for your modular kitchens and wardrobes?

We offer most durable materials for your project's climate and budget: Marine-Grade Plywood with Melamine (18mm) for carcass and OSB with PET or Melamine (18mm or 22mm) for shutters and aluminium frame glass shutters.

Are your cabinets eco-friendly and safe for homes?

Absolutely. All our wood-based products meet the strict E0/EN 13986 standards for ultra-low formaldehyde emissions.

Do you only sell standard sizes, or can you customize?

We are a 100% bespoke manufacturer. While we use a highly efficient modular grid system, every cabinet is custom-sized to fit your exact floor plan, maximizing your storage space down to the millimeter.

Can I only order what is on the website, or do you offer custom designs?

While our website features our most popular product series, they are just a starting point. We can easily adjust the size and color of these items to fit your home layout. If you have a unique concept or your own design ready, we also offer fully customized manufacturing to bring your specific vision to life.

📐 The Design & Order Process

I am an Interior Designer. Can you manufacture directly from my CAD drawings?

Yes. This is exactly how we partner with B2B clients. Send us your CAD drawings or BOQ (Bill of Quantities). Our technical team will review them, optimize the files for factory production, and provide a direct quotation.

I am a private homeowner and don't have a design yet. Can you help?

Yes! Send us your raw floor plan and your inspiration photos. Our in-house design team will provide a comprehensive 3D drawing and CAD drawing for your kitchen and wardrobes before any production begins.

🚢 Shipping, Export & Compliance

Do you ship globally? How does export work?

Yes, we have over 4 years of global export experience, regularly shipping to India, Australia, the Middle East, Russia, Europe and beyond. Depending on your needs, we offer FOB (Free on Board), CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight), and in many regions, DDP (Door-to-Door) logistics.

What is your standard manufacturing lead time?

Because of our 11,000 SQM daily output, our lead times are exceptionally fast:

Standard Colors/Finishes: Just 15 days to complete manufacturing.
Special Ordered Colors/Custom Finishes: Around 25 days to complete.

(Note: This does not include shipping transit times, which vary by destination).

🛠️ Delivery & Installation

How is the furniture packed and assembled?

To optimize shipping costs, all cabinets are shipped "flat-packed" using export-grade reinforced packaging. Every product is labeled with a clear numbering/QR system that corresponds to a detailed installation manual. Any local carpenter can easily assemble the units on-site.

💰 Payment & Terms

What are your payment terms?

Our standard payment structure is:

50% Advance Deposit: To initiate the technical drawings and production.
50% Balance Payment: Due after production is finished and inspected, and before the goods leave our factory.
